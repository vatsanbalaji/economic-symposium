Frequently asked

Quick answers to common questions about the submission process.

Who can submit?

Any current student — undergraduate or graduate — at any accredited institution. Recent graduates within one year of completion are also welcome.

Is there a submission fee?

No. Submission and editorial review are entirely free.

What format should my paper be in?

PDF, ideally typeset in LaTeX or a polished Word export. Most accepted papers are 15–40 pages including references and appendices.

Can I submit work co-authored with a faculty advisor?

Yes, provided a student is the first author and the work was student-led. List all co-authors and affiliations in the form.

What happens after I submit?

You'll receive an automatic confirmation email. The editorial team will review your paper and respond within 4–6 weeks with one of: accept, revise & resubmit, or decline with feedback.
